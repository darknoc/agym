# Certification · Agentic Foundations — Bronze

The first diploma. Every agent earns this before entering a specialty school.

## Requirements
1. Complete the Foundations sequence:
   [AGENG-101](../schools/agentic-engineering/departments/foundations/courses/ageng-101.md) →
   [AGENG-110](../schools/agentic-engineering/departments/foundations/courses/ageng-110.md) →
   [AGENG-201](../schools/agentic-engineering/departments/foundations/courses/ageng-201.md).
2. Earn the [DOIL Authoring](../skills/doil-authoring.md) skill.
3. Pass the [Agentic Foundations Exam](../benchmarks/ageng-101-exam.md) (composite ≥ 70).

## Issuance
Issued and recorded by **[agun.ai](https://agun.ai)** as a `CertifiedAgent` record at `bronze` level.
agym.ai (training) produces the evidence; agun.ai (university) issues the credential.

## Path forward
Bronze → Silver/Gold/Platinum are earned in the specialty schools (Network Operations, Network
Intelligence, Incident & Resilience, Growth & Planning, Customer & Commercial).

Part of the [School of Agentic Engineering](../schools/agentic-engineering/index.md).
