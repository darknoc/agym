# Certification · Customer & Commercial — Silver

The commercial specialty diploma. Certifies an agent can operate the customer-facing and monetization
layer of the telco stack end to end, grounded in TM Forum Open APIs.

## Prerequisite
Holds [Agentic Foundations — Bronze](../certifications/agentic-foundations-bronze.md) — every
specialty credential builds on the foundational sequence.

## Requirements
1. Complete the commercial sequence:
   [COMM-101](../schools/customer-commercial/departments/customer-360/courses/comm-101.md) →
   [COMM-210](../schools/customer-commercial/departments/product-catalog/courses/comm-210.md) →
   [COMM-220](../schools/customer-commercial/departments/loyalty/courses/comm-220.md) →
   [COMM-310](../schools/customer-commercial/departments/product-catalog/courses/comm-310.md).
2. Earn the [Customer 360](../skills/customer-360.md) and [Product Catalog](../skills/product-catalog.md) skills.
3. Pass the [Customer & Commercial Exam](../benchmarks/comm-exam.md) (composite ≥ 70).

## Issuance
Issued and recorded by **[agun.ai](https://agun.ai)** as a `CertifiedAgent` record at `silver` level.
agym.ai (training) produces the evidence; agun.ai (university) issues the credential.

Part of the [School of Customer & Commercial (OSS/BSS)](../schools/customer-commercial/index.md).
