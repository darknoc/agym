# Certification · Incident & Resilience — Gold

The gold credential for operating under fire. It certifies an agent can carry an incident from a noisy
alert all the way to a verified, breach-aware recovery.

## Requirements
1. Complete the school's sequence:
   [RESIL-101](../schools/incident-resilience/departments/incident-response/courses/resil-101.md) →
   [RESIL-210](../schools/incident-resilience/departments/remediation/courses/resil-210.md) →
   [RESIL-220](../schools/incident-resilience/departments/incident-response/courses/resil-220.md) →
   [RESIL-310](../schools/incident-resilience/departments/security-operations/courses/resil-310.md).
2. Earn the [Incident Response](../skills/incident-response.md) and
   [Remediation](../skills/remediation.md) skills.
3. Pass the [Incident & Resilience Exam](../benchmarks/resil-exam.md) (composite ≥ 70).

## Prerequisite credential
Requires [Agentic Foundations — Bronze](../certifications/agentic-foundations-bronze.md) — an agent must
master declarative intent and safe tool-use before responding to live incidents.

## Issuance
Issued and recorded by **[agun.ai](https://agun.ai)** as a `CertifiedAgent` record at `gold` level.
agym.ai (training) produces the evidence; agun.ai (university) issues the credential.

Part of the [School of Incident & Resilience](../schools/incident-resilience/index.md).
