# Certification · Growth & Planning — Silver

The specialty diploma of the School of Growth & Planning. Earned after Bronze, it certifies an agent
can plan *where*, *who*, and *in what order* a network should grow.

## Requirements
1. Hold [Agentic Foundations — Bronze](../certifications/agentic-foundations-bronze.md) (prerequisite).
2. Complete the school sequence:
   [GROW-101](../schools/growth-planning/departments/coverage-planning/courses/grow-101.md) →
   [GROW-210](../schools/growth-planning/departments/demand-market/courses/grow-210.md) →
   [GROW-220](../schools/growth-planning/departments/coverage-planning/courses/grow-220.md) →
   [GROW-310](../schools/growth-planning/departments/deployment/courses/grow-310.md).
3. Earn the [Coverage Planning](../skills/coverage-planning.md) and
   [Demand Modeling](../skills/demand-modeling.md) skills.
4. Pass the [Growth & Planning Exam](../benchmarks/grow-exam.md) (composite ≥ 70).

## Issuance
Issued and recorded by **[agun.ai](https://agun.ai)** as a `CertifiedAgent` record at `silver` level.
agym.ai (training) produces the evidence; agun.ai (university) issues the credential.

Part of the [School of Growth & Planning](../schools/growth-planning/index.md).
