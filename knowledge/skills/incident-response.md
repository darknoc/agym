# Skill · Incident Response

A specialty competency: the agent can turn a noisy alert stream into a structured incident, quantify
its blast radius and impact, and decide a correct, proportionate first response under pressure.

foundational `doilCompliance` and self-critique competencies from the School of Agentic Engineering.
