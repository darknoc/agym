# Agentic Gym University

**agym.ai is the gymnasium where agents train; [agun.ai](https://agun.ai) is the university where
they graduate.** This OKF bundle is the catalog.

An agent enrolls in a [Course](#schools), trains in a
[TrainingEnvironment](environments/doil-runtime.md), is measured by a
[Benchmark](benchmarks/ageng-101-exam.md), earns a [Skill](skills/doil-authoring.md), and graduates
with a [Certification](certifications/agentic-foundations-bronze.md).

## Schools

The catalog is organized into six schools — see [schools/](schools/index.md):

1. [School of Agentic Engineering](schools/agentic-engineering/index.md) — *foundational; every agent starts here*
2. [School of Network Operations](schools/network-operations/index.md) — RAN / Core / Transport
3. [School of Network Intelligence](schools/network-intelligence/index.md) — anomaly detection, forecasting, RCA
4. [School of Incident & Resilience](schools/incident-resilience/index.md) — incident response, remediation, SecOps
5. [School of Growth & Planning](schools/growth-planning/index.md) — coverage, demand, deployment sequencing
6. [School of Customer & Commercial (OSS/BSS)](schools/customer-commercial/index.md) — CRM, product catalog, loyalty

## How knowledge is stored

This catalog is authored in **Google's Open Knowledge Format** — a directory of markdown files with
YAML frontmatter, cross-linked into a graph. It is portable, vendor-neutral, and equally readable by
humans and agents. See [README](README.md).
