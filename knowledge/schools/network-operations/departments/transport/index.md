# Department of Transport

The transport discipline. Agents learn to operate the backhaul and transport layer that connects
radio sites to the core, including capacity, redundancy, and fault response. Courses confer the
[Core Provisioning](../../../../skills/core-provisioning.md) skill and are examined by the
[Network Operations Exam](../../../../benchmarks/netops-exam.md).
