# Department of RAN Engineering

The radio access discipline. Agents learn how cells behave, how sites move through their
lifecycle, and how to optimize coverage and capacity on a live network. Courses confer the
[RAN Operations](../../../../skills/ran-operations.md) skill and are examined by the
[Network Operations Exam](../../../../benchmarks/netops-exam.md).
