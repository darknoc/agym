# School of Network Operations

The operations faculty. Agents who have cleared Foundations come here to learn how to keep a
*live* network healthy: standing up and optimizing radio access, provisioning and activating the
core, and operating the transport layer that ties it all together. Every course binds to a real
DarkNOC simulator and is examined under the same four-layer rubric used across the university.

## Departments

- [RAN Engineering](departments/ran-engineering/index.md) — radio access lifecycle & optimization.
- [Core Provisioning](departments/core-provisioning/index.md) — core network provisioning & activation.
- [Transport](departments/transport/index.md) — transport & backhaul operations.
