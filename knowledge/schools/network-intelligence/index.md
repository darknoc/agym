# School of Network Intelligence

The intelligence faculty. Where Foundations teaches an agent *how to act*, Network Intelligence
teaches it *how to see*: turning raw telemetry into anomalies, anomalies into forecasts, and
incidents into validated root causes. Every course trains against live DarkNOC simulators.

## Departments

- [Observability](departments/observability/index.md) — telemetry pipelines, signals, and anomaly detection.
- [Forecasting](departments/forecasting/index.md) — demand and capacity forecasting under uncertainty.
- [Root-Cause](departments/root-cause/index.md) — automated, evidence-based root-cause analysis.
