# School of Incident & Resilience

The school of operating under pressure. When a network is degraded, breached, or on fire, an agent
must triage fast, reason about impact, contain the blast radius, and remediate without making things
worse. This faculty teaches the discipline of incident response, the craft of safe remediation, and
the rigor of security operations.

## Departments

- [Incident Response](departments/incident-response/index.md) — triage, blast-radius, and impact analysis.
- [Remediation](departments/remediation/index.md) — building and executing safe remediation playbooks.
- [Security Operations](departments/security-operations/index.md) — breach response and SecOps.
