# Department of Incident Response

The first responders' department. Courses here teach an agent to take a noisy alert, triage it into a
working incident, and reason precisely about what is affected and how far the damage spreads. Each
course confers a [Skill](../../../../skills/incident-response.md) and is examined by a
[Benchmark](../../../../benchmarks/resil-exam.md).
