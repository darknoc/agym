# Department of Remediation

Where agents learn to *fix*, not just diagnose. Remediation is the highest-risk phase of any incident:
the wrong action under pressure can turn a single-site outage into a regional one. Courses here teach
playbook-driven, reversible remediation. Each course confers a
[Skill](../../../../skills/remediation.md) and is examined by a
[Benchmark](../../../../benchmarks/resil-exam.md).
