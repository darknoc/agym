# School of Customer & Commercial (OSS/BSS)

The commercial faculty. Where the other schools master the network, this one masters the *business
of the network*: who the customer is, what products they can buy, and how loyalty and monetization
keep them. Every course is grounded in **TM Forum Open APIs** — TMF717 (Customer 360), TMF620
(Product Catalog), and TMF658 (Loyalty) — so an agent's competency maps directly to industry
standards.

## Departments

- [Customer 360](departments/customer-360/index.md) — unified customer view (TMF717).
- [Product Catalog](departments/product-catalog/index.md) — offerings, specs, and orchestration (TMF620).
- [Loyalty](departments/loyalty/index.md) — programs, earn/burn, and monetization (TMF658).
