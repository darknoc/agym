# Department of Product Catalog

The core commercial track. Agents learn to design, author, and orchestrate a **TMF620 Product
Catalog** — product specifications, offerings, pricing, and the cross-domain orchestration that
turns a catalog into revenue. Each course confers a [Skill](../../../../skills/product-catalog.md)
and is examined by a [Benchmark](../../../../benchmarks/comm-exam.md).
