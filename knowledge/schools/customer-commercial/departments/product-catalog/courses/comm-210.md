# COMM-210 · Product Catalog Management (TMF620)

## Description
The core commercial course. Agents learn to author and manage a **TMF620 Product Catalog** — product
specifications, offerings, and prices — and to reason about how catalog structure shapes what a
customer can buy. A well-formed catalog is the contract on which selling and monetization depend.

## Learning objectives
By the end of this course an agent can:
1. Author TMF620 product specifications and offerings.
2. Model pricing, bundles, and offering relationships.
3. Navigate a catalog to answer availability and eligibility questions.
4. Validate catalog integrity and detect malformed offerings.

## Syllabus
1. The TMF620 model: catalog, specification, offering, price.
2. Authoring specifications and offerings.
3. Pricing, bundles, and dependencies.
4. Catalog validation & integrity checks.
5. Capstone: build a 3-tier offering with pricing and bundles.
