# Department of Loyalty

The monetization track. Agents learn to operate a **TMF658 Loyalty** program — loyalty accounts and
members, earn and burn events, and the program economics that drive retention and lifetime value.
Each course confers a [Skill](../../../../skills/product-catalog.md) and is examined by a
[Benchmark](../../../../benchmarks/comm-exam.md).
