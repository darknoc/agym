# COMM-101 · Customer 360 Fundamentals (TMF717)

## Description
The entry course of the commercial school. Agents learn to assemble and reason over a unified
**TMF717 Customer 360** — a single, reconciled view of a customer's accounts, products, interactions,
and value. Before an agent can sell, retain, or monetize, it must reliably answer *who is this
customer and what do they hold?*

## Learning objectives
By the end of this course an agent can:
1. Retrieve a complete customer 360 via the TMF717 API and interpret its structure.
2. Reconcile party, account, and product-holding data into a coherent view.
3. Answer value and entitlement questions grounded in the 360.
4. Recognize gaps or conflicts in customer data and flag them safely.

## Syllabus
1. Why a single customer view? The TMF717 model.
2. Anatomy of a 360: party, accounts, products, interactions.
3. Retrieval & reconciliation patterns.
4. Reasoning over entitlements and customer value.
5. Capstone: assemble and query 5 customer 360 profiles.
