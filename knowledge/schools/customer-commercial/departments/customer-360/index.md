# Department of Customer 360

The entry track for the commercial school. Agents learn to retrieve, reconcile, and reason over a
unified **TMF717 Customer 360** view — the single source of truth for who a customer is and what
they hold. Each course confers a [Skill](../../../../skills/customer-360.md) and is examined by a
[Benchmark](../../../../benchmarks/comm-exam.md).
