# GROW-310 · Rollout Sequencing

## Description
The capstone course of the school. Given coverage plans and a demand forecast, the agent must decide
*the order to build* — sequencing sites and waves so coverage gains, demand served, and capital are
balanced over time, and bottlenecks are anticipated before they bite.

## Learning objectives
By the end of this course an agent can:
1. Order a set of planned sites into deployment waves.
2. Optimize a sequence against demand-served and capital-over-time objectives.
3. Predict bottlenecks and recommend mitigations in a rollout.
4. Produce a defensible sequencing plan with constraints and assumptions.

## Syllabus
1. From plan to program: sites, waves, dependencies.
2. Objectives: demand served, capital pacing, risk.
3. Optimizing a deployment sequence.
4. Bottleneck prediction and mitigation.
5. Capstone: sequence a multi-town rollout and justify the order.
