# School of Growth & Planning

The faculty of expansion. An agent here learns to answer *where* a network should grow, *who* it
should serve, and *in what order* it should be built: coverage and RF planning, demand and market
modeling, and deployment sequencing — grounded in geospatial, POI, and population data.

## Departments

- [Coverage Planning](departments/coverage-planning/index.md) — coverage, RF planning, site selection.
- [Demand & Market](departments/demand-market/index.md) — demand and market modeling.
- [Deployment](departments/deployment/index.md) — rollout sequencing and build order.
